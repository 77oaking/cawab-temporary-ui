Overview

A Unified Platform for Cantonmentians

CAWAB is a unified platform established for all former students of cantonment-based educational institutions — including Cantonment Public, Cantonment Board, Cantonment English medium, BAF Shaheen Colleges, and Bangladesh Navy (BN) Colleges. The association strengthens bonds among alumni, promotes mutual cooperation, and creates opportunities for networking, mentorship, and social development.

History

From BCCC to CAWAB

Originally established as the Bangladesh Cantonment College Community on 28 August 2020, the organization was renamed Bangladesh Cantonment Students' Community (BCSC) on 1 October 2021, expanding to 96 institutions. On 20 March 2025 the Founding Member Council approved 16 additional Navy and Air Force institutions, and in July 2025 it became Cantonmentians' Alliance & Welfare Association of Bangladesh (CAWAB).
