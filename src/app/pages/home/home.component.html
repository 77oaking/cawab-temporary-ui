Overview

Our Journey, Our Mission

CAWAB was founded on August 28, 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic by Md Shakawat Hossen HCPSC, with co-founders Wasif Ahmad Chowdhury HCPSC and Mohammed Sadman Sakib Chowdhury CCPC.

Initially named Bangladesh Cantonment College Community (BCCC), it was created to help students from cantonment colleges support each other.
